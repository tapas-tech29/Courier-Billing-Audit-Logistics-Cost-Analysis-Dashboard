 # 📦 Courier Billing Audit & Logistics Cost Analysis Dashboard



\

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI project focuses on auditing courier billing charges and analyzing logistics costs by comparing expected shipping charges with actual courier-billed amounts.

The dashboard helps identify:

* ✅ Correctly Charged Orders
* 🔺 Overcharged Orders
* 🔻 Undercharged Orders
* 📈 Billing Accuracy
* 🚚 Delivery Zone Performance

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Courier invoices often contain billing discrepancies due to incorrect weight calculations or delivery zone mappings. These discrepancies can increase logistics costs and impact profitability.

This dashboard helps verify courier invoices and identify billing errors using data-driven analysis.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards

* 📦 Total Orders Processed
* 💰 Total Invoice Amount
* ✅ Correctly Charged Orders
* 🔺 Overcharged Orders
* 🔻 Undercharged Orders
* 📉 Net Charge Difference

### Visualizations

#### 🍩 Order Billing Status Distribution

Displays the proportion of correctly charged, overcharged, and undercharged orders.

#### 📊 Orders Distribution by Delivery Zone

Analyzes shipment volume across delivery zones.

#### 📈 Overcharge vs Undercharge by Zone

Compares billing discrepancies across different delivery zones.

#### 🔥 Top 10 Overcharged Orders

Highlights the orders with the highest overcharge amounts.

#### 🎯 Billing Accuracy %

Measures the percentage of correctly billed orders.
